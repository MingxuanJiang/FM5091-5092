In the final project, it contains two projects - MonteCarlo_Calculator and PortfolioManager_MingxuanJiang. The former one is used to calculate the price and greeks through Monte Carlo Simulation. The later one is the trade interface.

Menu bar has three buttons: File, Analysis and Setting.

First, users can generate data into SQL through Setting--Generate Data. Then sample data is added. Also, users can add data through File--New. And then refresh and price the stock and options using simulation. Then, users can refresh the trade and get the greeks of stocks and options.
